Bank of Russia statistics

Number of banks in Russia from 2001 to 2020

According to the statistics of the Bank of Russia, the number of banks in the Russian Federation as of 01.01.2020 is 484, that is, over the past year their number has decreased by 42 (484 - 442 = 42). If we take statistics for the last 10 years, their number has decreased by more than half (as of 01.01.2009 - 1108 banks, as of 01.01.2020 - 442).
